Chicken Wrap

Chicken Wrap Ingredients

  • 4-6 oz cooked chicken breast or rotisserie chicken
  • Low Carb Tortilla
  • Spinach
  • Mozzeralla Cheese

    • Directions

  • Warm tortilla in toaster oven while also warming chicken in microwave for 1 minute
  • Place warm chicken in warm tortilla and top with spinach and mozzarella
  • Add favorite condiment, I personally use a mango habenero hot sauce
  • Enjoy!

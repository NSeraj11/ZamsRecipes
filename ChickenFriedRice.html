Chicken Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 4-8 oz cooked chicken breast
  • Already cooked white rice
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 whole egg
  • Mixed Veggies(Peas,Carrots)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

    • Directions

  • Season chicken with salt and pepper and cook on medium heat
  • Once chicken is mostly cooked, drop in mixed veggies as well as minced garlic
  • Once vegetables appear cooked, add in whole egg
  • add white rice(best if rice has been sitting in fridge) and mix
  • Add in desired amount of soy sauce while continuing to mix
  • Enjoy!

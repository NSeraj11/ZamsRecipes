Chicken Quesadilla

Ingredients

  • 4-6 oz cooked chicken breast or rotisserie chicken
  • Low Carb Tortilla
  • Mozzeralla Cheese

    • Directions

  • Spray pan with olive oil
  • Place tortilla on pan and fill half with mozzerella and chicken,fold over half when done
  • Cook over medium heat for 3 minutes, then flip and cook for additional 2 minutes
  • Enjoy!

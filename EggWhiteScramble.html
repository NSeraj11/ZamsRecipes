Egg White Scramble

Ingredients

  • 2 whole eggs
  • 6 tablespoons of Egg Whites
  • Mozzerella cheese(Optional)
  • Milk(Optional)

    • Directions

  • Combine all ingredients together
  • Let combination of ingredients cook over medium heat
  • Once eggs are mostly cooked, change heat to low and let eggs finish cooking
  • Take eggs of burner or add extra cheese if desired and stir
  • Enjoy!

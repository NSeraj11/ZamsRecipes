Hamburger

Ingredients

  • Beef Patties
  • Olive Oil Spray
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Sliced Cheddar Cheese
  • Sliced Romaine Lettuce
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Condiments

    • Directions

  • Spray cooking pan with olive oil
  • Season both sides of patties with salt and pepper and then cook on medium high heat
  • For medium rare, cook 3 minutes on first side and then flip over and cook for 2 more minutes
  • Place pattie on either toasted or non toasted hamburger bun
  • Top with lettuce,cheese, and any other condiments
  • Enjoy!
